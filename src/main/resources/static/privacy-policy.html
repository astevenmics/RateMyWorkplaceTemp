Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information RateMyWorkplace ("we", "us") collects when you use the site, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: name, display name, username, email address and password (stored as a salted hash, never in plain text).
  • Employment verification documents: offer letters, pay stubs or similar proof you upload to unlock feedback for a company. These are used only to confirm employment and are not published publicly.
  • Content you submit: ratings, written feedback, comments and any site feedback you send us.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, general traffic counters and basic device/browser information, collected to keep the service reliable and to understand aggregate usage.
  • Cookies: a session cookie to keep you signed in, and a local preference (e.g. light/dark theme) stored in your browser.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account, and to verify your employment before unlocking feedback.
  • To display your reviews and display name publicly, while keeping your real name and verification documents private.
  • To send account-related email, such as verification codes and password resets.
  • To detect abuse, enforce our Terms of Service, and maintain the security of the platform.
  • To improve the site based on aggregate, de-identified usage patterns.

3. What we don't do

We don't sell your personal information. We don't publish your verification documents, your real name, or your email address on the site — only your chosen display name is shown alongside feedback you post.

4. Sharing

We may share information with service providers who help us run the site (for example, email delivery), and we may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property or safety of RateMyWorkplace, our users, or the public.

5. Data retention

We retain account and content data for as long as your account is active. Verification documents are retained only as long as needed to confirm employment and are deleted or anonymized afterward on a routine basis.

6. Your choices

  • You can edit your display name and profile picture at any time from your profile.
  • You can request that your account and associated personal data be deleted by contacting us using the form in the site footer.
  • You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling essential cookies may prevent you from staying signed in.

7. Children's privacy

RateMyWorkplace is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older and is not directed at children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent using the feedback form in the site footer.
